Honoring Service: Roof On Texas - A Veteran-Owned Roofing Company

  • Patrick Moore
  • July 29, 2023

Introduction At Roof On Texas, we take immense pride in our legacy as a Veteran-Owned roofing company. Founded in 1996 and recently organized as an LLC, we are not just another roofing business; we are a team of skilled professionals with a shared dedication to excellence and a profound sense of duty. Our roots run deep, forged by years of honorable service to our nation. As a Disabled Veteran-Owned Company, we bring a unique commitment to integrity and a relentless pursuit of excellence to every roofing project we undertake.

Veteran-Owned Expertise

Our roofing services are anchored in decades of combined experience, backed by the disciplined values instilled in us through our military service. As veterans, we understand the significance of trust, honor, and dedication to the mission at hand. These core principles guide us in delivering top-quality roofing solutions to homeowners and businesses alike.


Quality Craftsmanship & Attention to Detail

Roof On Texas is committed to providing unparalleled craftsmanship and attention to detail. From roof installation to repair and replacement, we employ cutting-edge techniques and premium materials to ensure your roof stands strong against the elements. Our team's meticulous approach and precise execution guarantee a seamless roofing experience and long-lasting results.


Comprehensive Storm Mitigation
As veterans, we know the impact of severe weather on homes and communities. That's why Roof On Texas specializes in storm mitigation solutions, safeguarding your property against hurricanes, heavy winds, and hailstorms. Our state-of-the-art strategies and impact-resistant materials will protect your home, giving you peace of mind during challenging weather conditions.


A Personalized Approach

At Roof On Texas, we understand that every roofing project is unique. That's why we offer tailored solutions to suit your budget, style preferences, and specific requirements. Our team takes the time to listen to your needs and concerns, guiding you through the entire process, from initial consultation to project completion.


For more roofing tips and professional services, feel free to visit our website Roof On Texas and get in touch with our expert team today!


Conclusion

Choosing Roof On Texas means partnering with a roofing company that embodies the values of honesty, integrity, and dedication. Our Veteran-Owned roots empower us to provide roofing solutions built on excellence and a commitment to service. When you entrust your roofing needs to Roof On Texas, you are in capable hands. Experience the difference of working with a team of professionals who go above and beyond to protect and enhance your property. Contact us today and let us take care of your roofing needs with the honor and expertise of a true Veteran-Owned Company.
