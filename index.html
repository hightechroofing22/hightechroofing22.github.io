About Platinum Roofing Scot Ltd: Your Local Roofing Experts

At Platinum Roofing (Scot) Ltd, we’re proud to be central Scotland’s trusted roofing experts. Our fully qualified team handles everything from small repairs to full roof installations, always using high-quality materials for lasting results. Whether it’s flat or pitched roofs, slating, tiling, lead work, or chimney repairs, we treat your home as if it were our own and provide fast consultations with written quotes within two hours.

We’re committed to 100% customer satisfaction, reflected in our 5-star reviews on Facebook, MyBuilder, Checkatrade, and more. As a fully insured limited company, we offer a wide range of services including flat roofing systems, drone roof inspections, roughcasting, and sandstone repairs.
